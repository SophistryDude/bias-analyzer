## Content Availability by Platform (20-Year Window: 2006-2026)

### YouTube (2006-present)
- Most commentators have 5-15 years of video content
- Full transcripts available via captions/API
- Daily Wire, TYT, and independent creators: extensive back catalogs
- Cable news channels have YouTube presence since ~2010-2012

### Website Articles / RSS (varies)
- NYT, WaPo, WSJ: archives going back 20+ years, but paywalled
- AP, Reuters: extensive free archives
- Digital-native outlets (Vox, Axios, Politico): ~10-15 years
- Breitbart: since 2007
- HuffPost: since 2005
- Substack writers: since ~2019-2020

### Podcasts (limited history)
- Most political podcasts launched 2015-2020
- Joe Rogan: since 2009 (massive back catalog)
- Pod Save America: since 2017
- Ben Shapiro Show: since 2015
- Audio transcripts less readily available than video

### Cable News Transcripts
- Nexis/LexisNexis has transcripts going back 20+ years (paid)
- Limited free access to historical cable transcripts
- Show clips on YouTube provide partial coverage
